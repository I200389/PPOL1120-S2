Why is this a problem at HKUST? ▼
Excessive consumption of sugar-sweetened beverages (SSBs) is a critical public health issue. Driven by hectic schedules and emotional triggers, many students rely on cheap, convenient sugary drinks. High intake is independently associated with abdominal obesity, depression, and anxiety.
Ethical Policy Approach ▼
Our approach follows Beneficence and Respect for Autonomy. Instead of banning choices, we advocate for "Libertarian Paternalism"—using Nudging to make the healthy choice the easiest and most attractive option.

Proposed Solutions (NATO Framework)

O

Organisation: Water Fountains

Upgrading fountains to provide hot, warm, room, and cold water. In summer, free cold water acts as a "nudge" to choose hydration over expensive chilled sodas.

N

Nodality: Traffic Light System

Ranking drinks based on CFS health guidelines. Research shows this shifts sales from "Red" drinks to "Green" options by informing students instantly.
